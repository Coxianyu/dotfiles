*iostream*
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
*std::cout*
输出内容到屏幕上， 默认不换行， 换行符使用 |std::endl| 

example: 
>
    std::cout<<"this is test"<<endl;
    std::cout<<"this is" + var +"test"<<endl;
<
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
*std:cin*
从控制台读取变量
Example: 
>
    cin>>a;
<
